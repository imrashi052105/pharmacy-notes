# Human Anatomy & Physiology – Unit 1

## 🔹 Cell
- Cell = basic unit of life  
- Study of cell = Cytology  

### Parts of Cell:
1. Cell membrane  
   - Semi-permeable  
   - Controls entry & exit  

2. Cytoplasm  
   - Site of metabolic activities  

3. Nucleus  
   - Controls all activities  
   - Contains DNA  

---

## 🔹 Cell Transport

### Diffusion
- Movement from high → low concentration  
- No energy required  

### Osmosis
- Movement of water through semi-permeable membrane  

### Active Transport
- Movement from low → high concentration  
- Energy (ATP) required  

---

Types:
1. Epithelial
